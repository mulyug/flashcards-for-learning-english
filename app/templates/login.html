Learn English with flashcards

A simple flashcard app that helps you grow your English vocabulary with spaced repetition. Add the words you want to learn, then review them daily in short sessions.

  • 1 Build your own deck. Add the words and phrases you actually want to remember — together with their translation.
  • 2 Review every day. Rate each card as Hard, Medium, or Easy and let the app decide when to show it again.
  • 3 Focus on what's hard. Tricky words come back sooner, the ones you know stay out of your way.
